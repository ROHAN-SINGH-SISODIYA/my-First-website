Hostel

Descriptions

SISTec is well known for its extensive residential facilities. Students have the luxury of being located in the serene and green campus, in one of the finer suburbs of Bhopal, yet not far from the attractions of the city during weekends. Staying in the campus is particularly attractive because resident students have access to several college facilities. SISTec boasts 5 hostels; three at Gandhi Nagar campus and two at Ratibad campus. SISTec hostels are not ordinary hostels these are a way of life, a philosophy... a place of enthusiasm and vigour." Hostel rooms are well equipped with beds, mattress, study tables, wardrobes, 24 hours water supply, wi-fi and other allied amenities.
